FRIED RICE

Description

FRIED RICE is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a work or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafoods, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to other dishes. It is a traditional chinese preparation of cooked rice, vegetables, protein, soy sauce, and aromatics

Ingredients

  • 2 1\2 cups (500g) long grain perboiled rice
  • Vegetable oil
  • Chicken (Whole chicken or chicken drumsticks)
  • 1 tablespoon Nigerian curry powder
  • 1\2 cup og green beans and green peas
  • 1\2 cup od diced carrot
  • 1\2 cup of sweet corn
  • 3 tablespoons diced red bell pepper
  • salt
  • 2 onions
  • 3 stock cubes
  • 1 tablespoon of thyme

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice with cold watera and pour into a large pot. Place 1 cup (178 g) of dry rice into a fine mesh strainer. Run cold water over the rice and use your hands to gently rinse the rice. You can use Basmatti, white or jasmine rice for this recipe
  2. Add 2 cups (473 ml) of water to the large pot. When perboiling, use twice as much water as you do rice. in this case, use 2 cups (473ml) of water since you're perboiling 1 cup (178g) of dry rice.
  3. Boil the mixture over high heat for 5 minutes. Bring the water to a full rolling boil, then start your timer and let the rice boil for 5 minutes. Keep the lid off the pot while the liquid heats up so it doesn't boil over.
  4. Transfer the rice to a fine mesh strainer. Turn off the heat and hold a fine mesh strainer over your sink. Pour the mixture into the strainer to drain the water from the rice.
  5. Place the strainer in a bowl of cold water for about 5 minutes. Fill a large bowl with cold water, put the fine mesh strainer into the bowl of water so the rice can soak for several minutes.
  6. Put 1\2 cup (118ml) of stock in a large pot.
  7. Add the perboiled rice and the boiled mixture for 5 minutes and your other ingredients and seasonings alow it to cook for 25 minutes
