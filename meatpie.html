MEAT PIE

Description

The Nigerian meat pie is a delicious pastry filled with minced meat, potatoes and carrots. It is slighty flaky and very rich in flavor.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Potataoes
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Salt to taste
  • pepper
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Egg for brushing
  • 1 tablespoon of thyme

Steps

  1. mix the pastry in a large bowl, togethrr with flour , sugar, salt and baking powder. Add ubed butteer and rub it with the flour until you get a crumbly texture
  2. Beat the eggs with cold water and add it to the flour mixture, mix the dough until formed. Do not over mix, wrap the dough with cling firm
  3. Add your minced ingredients in the pie and bake in the oven for 25 minutes
